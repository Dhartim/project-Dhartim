Narrative

This Project will talk about data and visualization developed of endangered animal from International Union for Conservation of Nature (IUCN). Basically, there are many species which are threatened and IUCN has list of these species which are extinct, or critically extinct. My Goal is to show how many species are in red list in different categories globally and to show Different family / class of species are endangered. Since there are more than 100K species, here focus will be on species globally and specific class of species.

Figure is take from IUCN Red List. It shows number of species threatened globally divided into various Family
